[Pin] About Us

This game is a fan-made web game developed by me, an aspiring game dev you can call Alder, and the assistant game dev and lead artist Love. It aims to be a Digimon-themed pet simulator where you can collect, raise, and train Digimon. Additionally, you can name and run your very own Farm, play minigames in the Minigame Arcade, and even fight villains in the Story Mode. We hope you have an excellent time here on Digimon Digital Web!
